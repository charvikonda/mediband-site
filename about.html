About MediBand
Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2022 by visionary partners Charvi Konda and Nitya Shah, MediBand emerged from a shared commitment to making healthcare technology accessible to everyone, regardless of ability or condition.

The MediBand Story

It started with a simple question: "What if emergency responders could access critical medical information in seconds, not minutes?"

In 2022, partners Charvi Konda and Nitya Shah witnessed firsthand how precious time was lost during medical emergencies due to communication barriers and inaccessible medical records. They recognized that existing solutions weren't designed with accessibility and inclusivity as core principles.

Drawing from their backgrounds in healthcare technology and inclusive design, they founded MediBand with a revolutionary vision: create a smart wristband that could bridge the gap between emergency response and personal medical data, while ensuring no one was left behind due to disability, language barriers, or economic constraints.

From startup to healthcare revolution: What began as a DECA competition project has evolved into a comprehensive healthcare technology platform trusted by over 500 hospitals, 300 universities, and millions of users worldwide.

Our Journey

2022

Foundation & Vision

Charvi Konda and Nitya Shah founded MediBand, starting as a DECA competition project focused on inclusive emergency healthcare technology.
